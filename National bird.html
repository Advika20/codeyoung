National Bird

Common Name: Indian Peafoul Scientific Name: Pavo cristatus Adopted in: 1963 Found in: Indigenous to India, Nepal, Bangladesh, Myanmar, Sri Lanka but has been introduced all over the world Habitat: Grasslands, forests, near human habitats Eating Habits: Omnivorous Average weight: Male – 5 Kg; Female – 3.5 Kg Average Length: Male – 1.95 to 2.25 m; Female -upto0.95 m Average Wingspan: 1.8 m Average Lifespan: 15-20 years in wild Average Speed:13 km/h
